Why supporting us is important

Lake Kopa is more than just a body of water — it’s a place where people relax, spend time with family, and enjoy nature. But pollution, litter, and lack of proper care are slowly damaging the lake and the environment around it. Supporting the cleanup project helps protect local wildlife, restore clean water, and keep the area safe and beautiful for everyone. Every contribution makes a real difference: the more people join, the faster we can turn the lake back into a clean and healthy place. Donating isn’t just giving money — it’s taking action to protect nature and our future.
